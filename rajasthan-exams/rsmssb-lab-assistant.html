RSMSSB Lab Assistant Recruitment 2025 – Complete Details & Notification Updates

The Rajasthan Subordinate and Ministerial Services Selection Board (RSMSSB) conducts the Lab Assistant Exam to recruit eligible candidates in various government departments across Rajasthan. This recruitment drive is an excellent opportunity for individuals who have passed 10+2 (Science stream) and are aspiring for a secure government job in the laboratory field.

  • Exam Name: RSMSSB Lab Assistant Recruitment 2025
  • Conducting Authority: Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB)
  • Educational Qualification: 12th (Science) pass from a recognized board
  • Selection Process : Written Examination & Document Verification
  • Salary Structure:Selected candidates will be appointed under the Pay Matrix Level-8 of the 7th Pay Commission. The basic pay scale ranges between ₹5,200 to ₹20,200, along with a Grade Pay of ₹2,800. Additional allowances and benefits will be applicable as per Rajasthan Government rules.

RSMSSB Lab Assistant 2025: Apply Online – Step-by-Step Guide

The Rajasthan Subordinate and Ministerial Services Selection Board (RSMSSB) will soon begin the online application process for Lab Assistant (Prayogshala Sahayak) posts. All eligible and interested candidates can apply online through the official RSMSSB website – rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in.

How to Apply Online for RSMSSB Lab Assistant 2025?

Follow the steps below to fill the RSMSSB Lab Assistant Application Form 2025 correctly:

Step 1: Visit the Official Website

    • Go to the official RSMSSB portal : https://rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in

    Step 2 : Navigate to the Recruitment Section

    • On the homepage, click on the “Recruitment Advertisement” section.
    • Look for the Lab Assistant (Prayogshala Sahayak) 2025 Notification.

    Step 3 : Click on the Apply Online Link

    • You will be redirected to the Rajasthan SSO portal: https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in
    • If you don’t have an SSO ID, click on "Register" and create one using your Aadhaar, Facebook, or Google account.

    Step 4 : Fill the Application Form

    • Log in using your SSO ID and password.
    • Select the Lab Assistant 2025 Application Form.
  • Fill in all required personal, academic, and contact details correctly.

Step 5 : Upload Required Documents

    Upload scanned copies of the following documents in the correct format and size:

  • Passport-sized photograph
  • Signature
  • Educational certificates (10th, 12th)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile certificate
  • PwD/Ex-servicemen certificate (if applicable)

Step 6 : Pay the Application Fee

    Pay the application fee based on your category using any secure online payment method.

  • General / OBC (Creamy Layer) - ₹450/-
  • OBC (Non-creamy layer) / MBC / EWS - ₹350/-
  • SC / ST / PwD - ₹250/-
    • Note : Exact fee details will be confirmed once the official notification is released.

Step 7 : Submit and Print the Application

  • Double-check all the filled-in details.
  • Click “Submit” to finalize your application.
  • Download and print the application form and fee receipt for future reference.

RSMSSB Lab Assistant Complete Syllabus

General Knowledge & Current Affairs

  • Rajasthan History, Art, Culture, Literature & Heritage

    Ancient civilizations and major historical sites, Major dynasties and their achievements, Mughal-Rajput relations, Freedom movement and political awakening, Architecture, folk music, folk dances, fairs, festivals and folk deities, Prominent personalities of Rajasthan

  • Geography of Rajasthan

    Location, expansion and main physical regions, Climate, drainage system and multipurpose projects, Vegetation, wildlife conservation and agriculture

  • Indian History

    Ancient, Medieval and Modern Indian History, Indian National Movement, Important Historical Events

  • Indian Geography

    Physical Geography, Climate, Natural Resources, Agriculture, Industries, Demography

  • Indian Polity

    Constitution of India, Political System, Panchayati Raj, Public Policy, Rights Issues

  • Indian Economy

    Economic Development, Planning, Poverty, Inclusion, Demographics, Social Sector Initiatives

  • Current Affairs

    National and International Important Events, Sports, Awards and Honors, Books and Authors

General Science

  • Biology

    Genetics: Mendel's laws, DNA, RNA, chromosomes, Animal Kingdom: Classification, Human Systems: Digestive, respiratory, excretory, circulatory, nervous and reproductive systems, Economic importance of animals and biological evolution, Cell: Structure and cell division, Economic importance of plants and plant physiology, Biotechnology: Its application in agriculture and medicine

  • Physics

    Thermodynamics: Laws of thermodynamics, Electrostatics: Coulomb's law, electric field, Electric Current: Kirchhoff's laws, Optics: Microscope, telescope, interference, diffraction, Atom and Nuclei: Bohr's model of atom, nuclear binding energy, Semiconductor Electronics: PN junction, transistor, logic gates

  • Chemistry

    Atomic and Periodic Properties: Types of orbitals, atomic properties, Periodic table: S-block and P-block elements, Chemical and ionic equilibrium, Organic compounds: Nomenclature and general properties, States: Gaseous, liquid and solid state

  • Environmental Science

    Ecosystem, Biodiversity, Conservation, Environmental Pollution, Climate Change, Sustainable Development

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the educational qualification required for Rajasthan Lab Assistant?

Candidates must have passed 12th (10+2) with Science stream (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) from a recognized board. Some positions may require a diploma in relevant field.

What is the age limit for Rajasthan Lab Assistant exam?

The minimum age is 18 years and maximum age is 40 years as on 1st January 2025. Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per government rules.

Is there any negative marking in the exam?

Yes, there is negative marking of 1/3 mark for each wrong answer. No marks will be deducted for unattempted questions.

How can I download the admit card?

Admit cards can be downloaded from the official RSMSSB website using your registration number and password/date of birth. Admit cards are generally available 2-3 weeks before the exam.

What is the selection process for Rajasthan Lab Assistant?

The selection is based on written examination only. There is no interview for this position. The final merit list is prepared based on marks obtained in the written exam.

When will the result be declared?

The result is expected to be declared within 2-3 months after the examination. Candidates can check their results on the official RSMSSB website.
