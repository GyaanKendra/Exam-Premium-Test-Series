Patwari Recruitment 2025

Introduction to Patwari

The Patwari Recruitment 2025 offers excellent opportunities for graduates to work in the Revenue Department. Patwaris play a crucial role in maintaining land records, collecting land revenue, and assisting in various revenue administration functions.

Important: Patwaris serve as the backbone of the revenue administration system. They maintain crucial land records, handle mutations, resolve land disputes, and serve as the primary point of contact for land-related matters in their jurisdiction.

Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

  • Essential: Bachelor's degree from recognized university
  • Knowledge of local state language (Hindi/Rajasthani etc.)
  • Basic computer knowledge with certificate (6 months course)
  • Typing speed in local language (some states)

Age Limit (as on 01-01-2025)

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 40 years
  • Age relaxation as per government norms:
    • SC/ST: 5 years
    • OBC: 3 years
    • PWD: 10 years
    • Ex-servicemen: As per rules

Selection Process

The selection process for Patwari involves the following stages:

Stage Description Marks/Weightage
Written Examination Objective type questions on General Knowledge, Mathematics, Reasoning, Language, Computer Knowledge 100 marks
Document Verification Verification of educational certificates, age proof, caste certificate, computer certificate etc. Qualifying
Typing Test (if applicable) Typing test in local language (Hindi/Rajasthani) Qualifying

Note: The written examination is typically the main selection stage. Some states may have additional stages like interview or skill test. The exact selection process varies by state.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to the most common questions about Patwari Recruitment 2025:

What is the eligibility criteria for Patwari 2025?

Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university. Some states require knowledge of local language and computer skills. Age limit is typically 18-40 years with relaxation for reserved categories.

What is the selection process for Patwari?

The selection process typically involves written examination followed by document verification and sometimes interview or computer proficiency test.

What is the salary for Patwari?

The salary for Patwari typically falls in Pay Level 5 as per 7th Pay Commission, ranging from ₹25,500 to ₹81,100. This includes basic pay plus allowances like DA, HRA, and field allowance.

What are the key responsibilities of Patwari?

Key responsibilities include maintaining land records, collecting land revenue, updating mutation records, assisting in land surveys, and resolving land disputes.

Is there any physical test for Patwari?

Generally, there is no physical test for Patwari posts. The selection is based on written examination and academic qualifications.

What type of questions are asked in the written exam?

The written exam typically includes questions on general knowledge, mathematics, reasoning, language skills (Hindi/English), and computer knowledge.

Which departments recruit Patwaris?

Patwaris are recruited by State Revenue Departments under the Board of Revenue or respective state governments.

What is the career growth path for Patwari?

Patwari can progress to Naib Tehsildar, Tehsildar, and higher positions in the revenue department. With higher qualifications, they can become Revenue Officers.
