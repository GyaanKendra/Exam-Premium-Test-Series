Stenographer Recruitment 2024

Introduction to Stenographer

The Stenographer Recruitment 2024 offers excellent opportunities for candidates with typing and shorthand skills to build careers in government administration. Stenographers play a crucial role in maintaining official records, taking dictation, and ensuring efficient documentation in various government departments.

Important: Stenographers serve as essential support staff in government offices, courts, and ministries. They are responsible for accurate and speedy transcription of official documents, maintaining confidentiality, and assisting in administrative functions through their specialized skills in shorthand and typing.

Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

  • Essential: 12th class or equivalent from recognized board/university
  • Knowledge of English/Hindi language
  • Skill test qualification in stenography and typing
  • Computer proficiency certificate (preferred)

Age Limit (as on 01-01-2024)

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 27 years (SSC)
  • Age relaxation as per government norms:
    • SC/ST: 5 years
    • OBC: 3 years
    • PWD: 10 years
    • Ex-servicemen: As per rules

Skill Requirements

  • English Stenographer: 80 wpm dictation & 40 wpm typing speed
  • Hindi Stenographer: 35 wpm dictation & 30 wpm typing speed
  • Computer typing skills
  • Good listening and comprehension skills

Selection Process

The selection process for Stenographer involves the following stages:

Stage Description Marks/Weightage
Written Examination Computer Based Test (CBT) on General Intelligence, English, Quantitative Aptitude 200 marks
Skill Test - Stenography Dictation test in shorthand (English/Hindi) Qualifying
Skill Test - Typing Typing test on computer (English/Hindi) Qualifying
Document Verification Educational certificates, age proof, category certificate etc. Qualifying

Note: Candidates must qualify in both written examination and skill tests. Final merit is prepared based on written exam marks of candidates who qualify the skill tests.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to the most common questions about Stenographer Recruitment 2024:

What is the eligibility criteria for Stenographer 2024?

Candidates must have passed 12th class or equivalent from recognized board. For SSC Stenographer, age limit is 18-27 years. Typing speed of 80 wpm in English and 35 wpm in Hindi is required for different grades.

What is the selection process for Stenographer?

The selection process involves written examination (computer-based test) followed by skill test in stenography and typing. Final selection is based on combined performance in both stages.

What is the salary for Stenographer?

Stenographer Grade C salary ranges from ₹25,500 to ₹81,100, while Grade D ranges from ₹35,400 to ₹1,12,400 as per 7th Pay Commission, plus various allowances.

What are the key responsibilities of Stenographer?

Key responsibilities include taking dictation in shorthand, transcribing notes, typing official documents, maintaining records, file management, and assisting in administrative work.

What typing speed is required for Stenographer?

For English Stenographer: 80 wpm dictation and 40 wpm typing. For Hindi Stenographer: 35 wpm dictation and 30 wpm typing. Speed requirements may vary by organization.

What type of questions are asked in the written exam?

The written exam typically includes questions on general intelligence & reasoning, general awareness, English language & comprehension, and quantitative aptitude.

Which organizations recruit Stenographers?

Stenographers are recruited by SSC, State PSCs, Courts, Ministries, Railways, Defense departments, and various government offices across India.

What is the career growth path for Stenographer?

Stenographers can progress to Senior Stenographer, Personal Assistant, Private Secretary, and higher administrative positions through departmental promotions and examinations.
