Rajasthan CET Graduation Exam Preparation Strategy

Time Management Strategy

Create a realistic study schedule that covers all subjects. Allocate more time to difficult topics and revise regularly. Practice time-bound mock tests to improve speed.

Subject-wise Preparation Tips

Focus on General Knowledge and Mathematics as they carry more weightage. For languages, focus on grammar and vocabulary. For Reasoning, practice regularly.

Important Books and Resources

Refer to standard books for each subject. Use previous year question papers and mock tests. Follow reliable sources for current affairs and Rajasthan GK.

Revision Strategy

Make short notes for quick revision. Revise regularly, especially formulas, important dates, and facts. Solve previous year papers to understand the exam pattern.

Mock Test Strategy

Take regular mock tests to assess preparation. Analyze performance and work on weak areas. Practice with OMR sheets to get familiar with the exam pattern.

Last Minute Preparation Tips

Revise important formulas, facts, and current affairs. Avoid learning new topics. Stay calm and confident. Get proper rest before the exam.

What is the syllabus for Rajasthan CET Graduation exam?
The Rajasthan CET Graduation exam syllabus includes General Knowledge, General Science, Mathematics, Hindi, English, Computer Knowledge, and Reasoning. Detailed syllabus is available on this page.
What is the exam pattern for Rajasthan CET Graduation?
The exam consists of one paper with 150 multiple-choice questions carrying 150 marks. The duration is 2 hours. There is no negative marking in the exam.
When will the Rajasthan CET Graduation exam be conducted?
The Rajasthan CET Graduation exam is expected to be conducted in February 2026. Exact dates will be announced by Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education.
How to download Rajasthan CET Graduation admit card?
Admit cards will be available on the official website of Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education. Candidates need to login with their registration details to download the admit card.
