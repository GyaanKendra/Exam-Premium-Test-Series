    History, Culture, Geography, Economy, Polity of Rajasthan, Current Affairs of Rajasthan

  • Current Affairs

    National and International Important Events, Sports, Awards and Honors, Books and Authors

General Science

  • Physics

    Laws of Motion, Work, Energy and Power, Gravitation, Properties of Matter, Heat, Light, Sound, Electricity and Magnetism

    Elements, Compounds, Mixtures, Metals and Non-metals, Carbon and its Compounds, Acids, Bases and Salts

    Cell Structure, Genetics, Human Physiology, Plant Physiology, Diseases, Nutrition, Health and Hygiene

Hindi

Rajasthan Patwari Exam Preparation Strategy

Time Management Strategy

Create a realistic study schedule that covers all subjects. Allocate more time to difficult topics and revise regularly. Practice time-bound mock tests to improve speed.

Subject-wise Preparation Tips

Focus on Rajasthan-specific GK, current affairs, and basic mathematics. For reasoning, practice regularly. For languages, focus on grammar and vocabulary.

Important Books and Resources

Refer to standard books for each subject. Use previous year question papers and mock tests. Follow reliable sources for current affairs and Rajasthan GK.

Revision Strategy

Make short notes for quick revision. Revise regularly, especially formulas, important dates, and facts. Solve previous year papers to understand the exam pattern.

Mock Test Strategy

Take regular mock tests to assess preparation. Analyze performance and work on weak areas. Practice with OMR sheets to get familiar with the exam pattern.

Last Minute Preparation Tips

Revise important formulas, facts, and current affairs. Avoid learning new topics. Stay calm and confident. Get proper rest before the exam.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility criteria for Rajasthan Patwari exam?
The eligibility criteria for Rajasthan Patwari exam includes:
  • Candidate must be a citizen of India
  • Age limit: 18 to 40 years (age relaxation for reserved categories)
  • Educational qualification: 12th pass from recognized board
  • Knowledge of Hindi written in Devnagri script
What is the salary of Rajasthan Patwari?
The salary of Rajasthan Patwari is as per the pay scale of Level-5 (Rs. 21,700 - Rs. 69,100) of the 7th Pay Commission. In addition to basic pay, various allowances like DA, HRA, etc. are also provided.
