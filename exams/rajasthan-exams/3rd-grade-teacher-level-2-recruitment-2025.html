RSSB 3rd Grade Teacher Level-2 Recruitment 2025 – Apply Online for 2123 Vacancies, Check Exam Date & Syllabus

Big Update for Teaching Aspirants! Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has released the official notification for Rajasthan 3rd Grade Teacher Level-2 Recruitment 2025. A total of 2123 vacancies are available for Higher Primary School Teacher (Class 6 to 8). The online application process starts from 07 November 2025, and the RSSB Level-2 Exam 2026 will be conducted from 17–21 January 2026. Check complete details about eligibility, exam pattern, syllabus PDF, and admit card download at rssb.rajasthan.gov.in.

RSSB 3rd Grade Teacher Level-2 Recruitment 2025 Overview

Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has announced a massive recruitment drive for 2123 posts of 3rd Grade Teacher Level-2 (Higher Primary School Teacher for Class 6-8). This is a golden opportunity for teaching aspirants looking for a government job in Rajasthan's education department. The recruitment includes posts for various subjects including Sanskrit, Hindi, English, Social Science, and Mathematics-Science.

RSSB 3rd Grade Teacher Level-2 Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

The educational qualification varies based on the subject applied for:

Sanskrit
Other Subjects

For Sanskrit Subject:

  • Shastri (or equivalent traditional Sanskrit examination) and 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education
  • OR Shastri with at least 50% marks and 1-year B.Ed.
  • OR Shastri with at least 45% marks and 1-year B.Ed. (as per NCTE norms)
  • OR Varishtha Upadhyay with at least 50% marks and 4-year B.El.Ed.
  • OR Varishtha Upadhyay with at least 50% marks and 4-year Shastri-ShikshaShastri/B.A. Ed.

For Hindi, English, Social Science, Mathematics-Science Subjects:

  • Graduation and 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education
  • OR Graduation with at least 45% marks and 1-year B.Ed. (as per NCTE norms)
  • OR Senior Secondary with at least 50% marks and 4-year B.El.Ed.
  • OR Senior Secondary with at least 50% marks and 4-year B.A./B.Sc.Ed. or B.A.Ed./B.Sc.Ed.
  • OR Post-Graduation with minimum 55% marks and three-year integrated B.Ed.-M.Ed.

Additional Requirement: Must have passed Rajasthan Teacher Eligibility Test (REET) Level-2 in the concerned subject with minimum qualifying marks as per category.

Age Limit (as on 01 January 2026)

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 40 years
  • Age relaxation as per government rules for reserved categories

How to Apply for RSSB 3rd Grade Teacher Level-2 Recruitment 2025?

  1. Visit the official website: rssb.rajasthan.gov.in
  2. Complete One Time Registration (OTR) if not done already
  3. Login with SSO ID and password
  4. Click on "Apply Online" for 3rd Grade Teacher Level-2 Recruitment 2025
  5. Fill the application form with all required details
  6. Upload necessary documents and photograph
  7. Pay the application fee
  8. Submit the application and take a printout for future reference

Application Fee

Category Fee
General / Creamy Layer OBC/MBC ₹600
Non-Creamy Layer OBC/MBC, EWS, SC, ST ₹400
Persons with Disabilities (PwD) ₹400

Important Note: Candidates must have passed Rajasthan Teacher Eligibility Test (REET) Level-2 in the concerned subject with minimum qualifying marks as per their category. The minimum qualifying percentage varies from 36% to 60% depending on the category.

RSSB 3rd Grade Teacher Level-2 Salary

Selected candidates will be appointed as probationary trainees for 2 years. After satisfactory completion of probation period, they will be placed in Pay Matrix Level-10 as per 7th Pay Commission. During probation period, they will receive fixed stipend without any additional allowances.
