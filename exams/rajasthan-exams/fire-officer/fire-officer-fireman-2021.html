Assistant Fire Officer & Fireman Recruitment 2021

Introduction to Fire Service Recruitment

The Fire Service Recruitment 2021 for Assistant Fire Officer and Fireman positions offers an excellent opportunity for candidates seeking a challenging and rewarding career in public service. Fire service personnel play a crucial role in saving lives and property during emergencies.

Important: Fire Service jobs require physical fitness, mental alertness, courage, and quick decision-making abilities. These positions offer job security, attractive salary packages, and opportunities for professional growth.

Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

  • Assistant Fire Officer: Bachelor's degree from recognized university
  • Fireman: 10+2 or equivalent from recognized board
  • Knowledge of local state language
  • Basic computer knowledge preferred

Age Limit (as on 01-01-2021)

  • Assistant Fire Officer: 21 to 30 years
  • Fireman: 18 to 25 years
  • Age relaxation as per government norms:
    • SC/ST: 5 years
    • OBC: 3 years
    • PWD: 10 years
    • Ex-servicemen: As per rules

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to the most common questions about Fire Service Recruitment 2021:

What is the eligibility criteria for Assistant Fire Officer 2021?

Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree from recognized university. For Fireman, 10+2 or equivalent is required. Age limit is 18-25 years for Fireman and 21-30 years for Assistant Fire Officer with relaxation for reserved categories.

What is the physical standard for Fireman recruitment?

For Male candidates: Height - 165 cm, Chest - 81-86 cm. For Female candidates: Height - 157 cm. Physical tests include running, long jump, high jump, and rope climbing with specific time/distance requirements.

What is the selection process for Fire Service?

The selection process includes written examination, physical efficiency test, physical standard test, medical examination, and interview/document verification. Physical tests are qualifying in nature.

What is the salary for Assistant Fire Officer?

Assistant Fire Officer salary ranges from ₹35,400 to ₹1,12,400 as per 7th Pay Commission. Fireman salary ranges from ₹21,700 to ₹69,100. Both positions include various allowances and benefits.

Is there any height relaxation for Fireman?

Yes, height relaxation of 5 cm is provided for candidates from hilly areas and tribal regions. Some states also provide relaxation for SC/ST candidates as per government norms.

What type of questions are asked in written exam?

The written exam typically includes questions from General Knowledge, Reasoning, Mathematics, English/Hindi language, and basic science. For Assistant Fire Officer, questions may include fire safety and management topics.

Can female candidates apply for Fireman post?

Yes, female candidates can apply for Fireman posts. There are separate physical standards and sometimes separate vacancies for female candidates in fire services.

What is the career growth in Fire Service?

Fireman can be promoted to Leading Fireman, Driver, Sub Officer, Station Officer, and higher ranks. Assistant Fire Officer can progress to Divisional Officer, Deputy Chief Fire Officer, and Chief Fire Officer.
