Rajasthan Forest Guard Recruitment 2020

Forest Department Recruitment : Introduction

The Forest Department Recruitment 2020 offered a significant and eagerly awaited opportunity for thousands of candidates seeking a career in forest conservation and wildlife protection. This prestigious recruitment drive, which has now concluded, was a landmark event for professionals passionate about protecting and managing the country's natural resources.

The Forester and Forest Guard positions attracted a large number of applicants, each eager to contribute directly to environmental preservation and biodiversity protection. Candidates viewed these positions not just as jobs, but as an opportunity to make a meaningful impact in the conservation sector.

If you are passionate about wildlife conservation, forestry management, and environmental sustainability, the Forest Department offers a dynamic career path. These positions are integral to managing and safeguarding the country’s rich biodiversity and ensuring sustainable forest practices.

Important: Forest Department jobs require physical fitness, love for nature, and dedication to environmental conservation. These positions offer job security, attractive salary packages, and opportunities to work in natural environments.

RSMSSB वनरक्षक और वनपाल भर्ती 2020: शैक्षणिक योग्यता व आयु सीमा

राजस्थान कर्मचारी चयन बोर्ड (RSMSSB) द्वारा आयोजित वनपाल (Forester) और वनरक्षक (Forest Guard) भर्ती 2020 के लिए पात्रता मानदंड निम्नलिखित थे:

Education Qualification

  • वनपाल (Forester): उम्मीदवार का किसी मान्यता प्राप्त बोर्ड से 12वीं कक्षा उत्तीर्ण
  • वनरक्षक (Forest Guard): उम्मीदवार का किसी मान्यता प्राप्त बोर्ड से 10वीं कक्षा उत्तीर्ण
  • अन्य आवश्यकताएं:
    • देवनागरी लिपि में हिंदी का कार्यसाधक ज्ञान।
    • राजस्थान की संस्कृति का ज्ञान।

Age Limit(1 जनवरी 2021 की स्थिति में)

  • वनपाल: न्यूनतम 18 वर्ष और अधिकतम 40 वर्ष।
  • वनरक्षक: न्यूनतम 18 वर्ष और अधिकतम 24 वर्ष।

आयु में छूट (सरकारी नियमानुसार)

  • OBC वर्ग: पुरुषों को 5 वर्ष और महिलाओं को 10 वर्ष की छूट।
  • SC/ST वर्ग: पुरुषों को 5 वर्ष और महिलाओं को 10 वर्ष की छूट।
  • EWS वर्ग: पुरुषों को 5 वर्ष और महिलाओं को 10 वर्ष की छूट।
  • सामान्य वर्ग की महिलाएं: 5 वर्ष की छूट।
  • भूतपूर्व सैनिक, दिव्यांगजन आदि: राज्य सरकार के नियमानुसार छूट का प्रावधान।

यदि आप वन विभाग भर्ती राजस्थान के लिए तैयारी कर रहे हैं, तो इन पात्रता मानदंडों की गहराई से जांच करें और सुनिश्चित करें कि आप सभी शर्तों को पूरा करते हैं।

राजस्थान वन विभाग भर्ती 2020: शारीरिक दक्षता परीक्षण (PET)

Forest Guard और Forester दोनों पदों के लिए Physical Efficiency Test चयन प्रक्रिया का अनिवार्य भाग था। नीचे पदानुसार सभी शारीरिक दक्षता परीक्षणों का विस्तृत विवरण दिया गया है:

1️⃣ पैदल चाल (Walking Test)

यह परीक्षा दोनों पदों (वनपाल और वनरक्षक) के लिए अनिवार्य थी।

वनरक्षक पद के लिए, पैदल चाल के अतिरिक्त निम्नलिखित परीक्षण आयोजित किए गए:

  • शारीरिक मानक: ऊंचाई और सीना मापन।
  • दक्षता परीक्षण: पैदल चाल, सिट-अप्स (पुरुष), खड़ी लंबी कूद/गोला फेंक (महिला)।

  • शारीरिक और मानसिक रूप से पद के लिए फिटनेस की जांच।
  • योग्यता-आधारित चरण।

  • केवल सत्यापित उम्मीदवार अंतिम चयन सूची में।

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to the most common questions about Forest Department Recruitment 2020:

What is the eligibility criteria for Forest Guard 2020?

Candidates must have passed 10+2 or equivalent from recognized board. For Forester, Bachelor's degree in Science preferably with Forestry is required. Age limit is 18-25 years for Forest Guard and 21-30 years for Forester with relaxation for reserved categories.

What is the physical standard for Forest Guard recruitment?

For Male candidates: Height - 163 cm, Chest - 79-84 cm. For Female candidates: Height - 150 cm. Physical tests include running, long jump, high jump, and other physical activities with specific requirements.

What is the selection process for Forest Department?

The selection process includes written examination, physical efficiency test, physical standard test, medical examination, and interview/document verification. Physical tests are qualifying in nature.

What is the salary for Forest Guard?

Forest Guard salary ranges from ₹21,700 to ₹69,100 as per 7th Pay Commission. Forester salary ranges from ₹35,400 to ₹1,12,400. Both positions include various allowances and benefits.

Is there any height relaxation for Forest Guard?

Yes, height relaxation of 5 cm is provided for candidates from hilly areas and tribal regions. Some states also provide relaxation for SC/ST candidates as per government norms.

What type of questions are asked in written exam?

The written exam typically includes questions from General Knowledge, Reasoning, Mathematics, English/Hindi language, and environmental science/forestry topics.

Can female candidates apply for Forest Guard post?

Yes, female candidates can apply for Forest Guard posts. There are separate physical standards and sometimes separate vacancies for female candidates in forest departments.

What is the career growth in Forest Department?

Forest Guard can be promoted to Forester, Deputy Ranger, Ranger, and higher positions. Forester can progress to Deputy Conservator of Forests, Conservator of Forests, and Chief Conservator of Forests.
