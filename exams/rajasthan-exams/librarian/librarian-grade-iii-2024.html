Librarian Grade III Recruitment 2024

Introduction to Librarian Grade III

The Librarian Grade III Recruitment 2024 offers excellent opportunities for library science graduates to build careers in educational institutions and government libraries. Librarian Grade III professionals play a crucial role in managing information resources, assisting users, and maintaining organized knowledge systems.

Important: Librarian Grade III serve as essential information professionals in educational institutions and public libraries. They are responsible for organizing knowledge resources, providing reference services, managing digital libraries, and ensuring efficient access to information for students, researchers, and the general public.

Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

  • Essential: Bachelor's degree in Library Science (BLib/B.Lib.Sc.) with minimum 55% marks
  • OR: Master's degree in Library Science (MLib/M.Lib.Sc.)
  • Diploma in Library Science from recognized institution
  • Knowledge of computer applications in libraries
  • Familiarity with classification systems (DDC, UDC, CC)

Age Limit (as on 01-01-2024)

  • Minimum Age: 21 years
  • Maximum Age: 40 years
  • Age relaxation as per government norms:
    • SC/ST: 5 years
    • OBC: 3 years
    • PWD: 10 years
    • Ex-servicemen: As per rules

Additional Requirements

  • Knowledge of library automation software
  • Familiarity with digital library concepts
  • Basic computer proficiency
  • Good communication skills

Selection Process

The selection process for Librarian Grade III involves the following stages:

Stage Description Marks/Weightage
Written Examination Theory papers on Library Science, General Knowledge, and Reasoning 100 marks
Computer Proficiency Test Practical test on library software and computer applications 50 marks
Interview Assessment of subject knowledge and communication skills 25 marks
Document Verification Educational certificates, experience certificates, age proof etc. Qualifying

Note: The selection process may vary by state and organization. Some recruitment bodies may have different weightage for different stages or additional requirements like practical demonstration of cataloging skills.

Rajasthan Librarian 3rd Grade Exam Pattern

The Rajasthan Librarian Exam Pattern 2025, designed by the Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB), is structured to thoroughly evaluate candidates appearing for the RSMSSB Librarian Grade 3 Recruitment. The examination consists of two papers—Paper I and Paper II—each comprising 100 multiple-choice questions (MCQs), with a total of 200 marks and a duration of 120 minutes per paper. Paper I focuses on testing candidates' understanding of basic library science concepts, general knowledge of Rajasthan, and foundational librarian skills. In contrast, Paper II emphasizes advanced library science, information systems, and knowledge management, ensuring a well-rounded assessment of both core and specialized competencies essential for the Rajasthan Librarian 3rd Grade Exam 2025.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to the most common questions about Librarian Grade III Recruitment 2024:

What is the eligibility criteria for Librarian Grade III 2024?

Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree in Library Science (BLib) or equivalent with minimum 55% marks. Some states require Master's degree in Library Science. Age limit is typically 21-40 years with relaxation for reserved categories.

What is the selection process for Librarian Grade III?

The selection process typically involves written examination followed by interview and document verification. Some states may include computer proficiency test or practical demonstration.

What is the salary for Librarian Grade III?

The salary for Librarian Grade III typically falls in Pay Level 6 as per 7th Pay Commission, ranging from ₹35,400 to ₹1,12,400. This includes basic pay plus allowances like DA, HRA, and library allowance.

What are the key responsibilities of Librarian Grade III?

Key responsibilities include library management, cataloging, classification, reference services, digital library management, user assistance, acquisition of books, and maintaining library records.

Is computer knowledge required for Librarian Grade III?

Yes, basic computer knowledge and familiarity with library management software is essential. Many recruitment processes include computer proficiency tests.

What type of questions are asked in the written exam?

The written exam typically includes questions on library science fundamentals, classification systems, cataloging, information technology, general knowledge, and reasoning.

Which departments recruit Librarian Grade III?

Librarian Grade III are recruited by Education Departments, Universities, Colleges, Public Libraries, Research Institutions, and various government departments.

What is the career growth path for Librarian Grade III?

Librarian Grade III can progress to Librarian Grade II, Grade I, Deputy Librarian, University Librarian, and higher positions through departmental promotions and experience.
