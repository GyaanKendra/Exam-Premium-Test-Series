Patwar Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • Candidates must have graduated from a recognised university or board.
  • Knowledge of Hindi written in Devanagari script
  • Knowledge of Rajasthani culture
  • Basic computer knowledge preferred for some positions

Age Limit (as on 01-01-2026)

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 40 years
  • Age relaxation as per Rajasthan government norms:
    • SC/ST: 5 years
    • OBC: 5 years
    • PWD: 10 years
    • Ex-servicemen: As per rules
    • Rajasthan Domicile: Additional relaxation

Patwari 2025 Syllabus

Exam Authority: Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB)

The Rajasthan Patwari 2025 written examination tests candidates on fundamental subjects and general aptitude required for the administrative position. Understanding the complete Patwari syllabus 2025 is crucial for effective preparation strategy and scoring high marks in this competitive exam.

Detailed Patwari Syllabus 2025 - Subject-wise Breakdown

The Rajasthan Patwari exam syllabus 2025 covers four main subjects with specific topics that candidates must master for success.

General Knowledge & Current Affairs

Weightage: ~25-30 questions
  • Current Affairs (National & International events)
  • Indian History & Culture with focus on Ancient, Medieval, and Modern periods
  • Indian Geography including Physical, Economic, and Social Geography
  • Indian Polity & Constitution with emphasis on governance system
  • Indian Economy and economic development in India
  • General Science including Physics, Chemistry, and Biology fundamentals
  • Sports & Awards - National and International recognition
  • Rajasthan Specific Knowledge - History, Culture, Geography, Economy, and Polity of Rajasthan
  • Important Dates and days of national and international significance

Preparation Tips:

Focus on daily current affairs, Rajasthan-specific knowledge, and regular revision of static GK topics.

Mathematics & Quantitative Aptitude

Weightage: ~25-30 questions
  • Number System and basic arithmetic operations
  • Simplification using BODMAS rules
  • Percentage, Ratio & Proportion applications
  • Average and its practical applications
  • Profit, Loss & Discount calculations
  • Time, Work & Distance problems
  • Simple & Compound Interest calculations
  • Mensuration & Geometry - area, volume, and perimeter calculations
  • Data Interpretation from tables, graphs, and charts
  • Fundamental Algebra concepts and equations

Preparation Tips:

Practice daily with shortcuts, focus on accuracy, and solve previous years' question papers.

Reasoning & Mental Ability

Weightage: ~20-25 questions
  • Verbal & Non-Verbal Reasoning problems
  • Analogies & Classification of words and figures
  • Series Completion - number, letter, and mixed series
  • Coding-Decoding patterns and sequences
  • Blood Relations and family tree problems
  • Direction Sense and distance calculations
  • Logical Venn Diagrams and set theory applications
  • Statement & Conclusions analysis
  • Puzzles and seating arrangement problems
  • Mathematical Operations and symbol interpretations

Preparation Tips:

Develop logical thinking, practice regularly with different question patterns, and improve speed.

Language Proficiency (Hindi & English)

Weightage: ~20-25 questions
  • Grammar & Vocabulary in both Hindi and English
  • Comprehension passages with questions
  • Error Detection in sentences
  • Synonyms & Antonyms of common words
  • One Word Substitution and idioms/phrases
  • Sentence Formation and rearrangement
  • Translation between Hindi and English
  • Essay Writing on current topics
  • Fill in the Blanks with appropriate words
  • Miscellaneous - active/passive voice, direct/indirect speech

Preparation Tips:

Read newspapers daily, practice grammar rules, and build vocabulary through regular reading.

Important Notes for Patwari 2025 Preparation

  • The Rajasthan Patwari syllabus 2025 may have slight variations as per official notification
  • Focus on Rajasthan-specific topics in General Knowledge section
  • Practice with previous years' question papers to understand exam pattern
  • Time management is crucial during the examination
  • Regular revision is key to retaining information
