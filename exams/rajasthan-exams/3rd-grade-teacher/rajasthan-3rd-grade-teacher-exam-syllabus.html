REET Exam Pattern 2025: Complete Analysis

The Rajasthan 3rd Grade Teacher Examination 2025 follows a structured pattern with 150 questions carrying 2 marks each, totaling 300 marks. The exam duration is 2 hours and 30 minutes, and it includes negative marking where 1/3 mark is deducted for each incorrect response. Accuracy is therefore crucial for maximizing scores.

The examination consists of two papers - Paper I and Paper II - covering various subjects as per the official syllabus. Understanding this detailed exam pattern is essential for candidates aiming to succeed in the Rajasthan 3rd Grade Teacher Recruitment 2025.

REET Mains Syllabus 2025: Frequently Asked Questions

How to become a Grade 3 teacher in Rajasthan?

To become a Grade 3 teacher in Rajasthan, candidates must first complete a Bachelor's Degree along with a Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed.) or a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.). The next step is to qualify for the Rajasthan Eligibility Examination for Teachers (REET), which is mandatory. After clearing REET, candidates can apply for the Rajasthan 3rd Grade Teacher recruitment when vacancies are announced. They will need to pass a written examination conducted by the Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (BSER). Successful candidates then undergo document verification, after which a merit list is prepared based on their exam performance and REET score.

Who is eligible for 3rd Grade Teacher in Rajasthan 2025?

According to the Rajasthan 3rd Grade Teacher Eligibility Criteria 2025, the minimum age for recruitment is 18 years, while the maximum age limit is 40 years. Candidates from reserved categories are eligible for age relaxation as per applicable government rules. Additionally, candidates must possess the required educational qualifications and REET certification for the respective level they are applying for.

Is REET compulsory for Rajasthan 3rd Grade Teacher recruitment?

Yes, REET qualification is mandatory for Rajasthan 3rd Grade Teacher recruitment. REET is a state-level competitive examination organized by the Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (BSER) to recruit qualified candidates for Grade III teacher positions in government schools across Rajasthan. It serves as an essential eligibility criterion and is conducted annually or as needed based on vacancy requirements.

What is the difference between Level 1 and Level 2 in REET Mains?

REET Level 1 is for candidates aspiring to teach classes 1 to 5 (primary level), while REET Level 2 is for those aiming to teach classes 6 to 8 (upper primary level). The syllabus and exam pattern differ accordingly, with Level 2 having more emphasis on subject specialization. Candidates can appear for both levels if they meet the eligibility criteria for each.

When will the Rajasthan 3rd Grade Teacher exam be conducted?

The official exam dates for Rajasthan 3rd Grade Teacher recruitment 2025 have not been announced yet. Candidates are advised to regularly check the official website of the Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (BSER) for updates regarding examination schedule, application dates, and other important notifications.
