Hostal Superintendent Grade II (SJED) 2024

Rajasthan Hostal Superintendent Grade II

The Hostal Superintendent Grade II recruitment under SJED (State Jurisdiction Education Department) is a prestigious opportunity for candidates seeking a career in educational administration and hostel management. This position plays a crucial role in maintaining discipline, ensuring student welfare, and managing hostel facilities in educational institutions.

Important: The Hostal Superintendent is responsible for the overall administration of hostels, including student accommodation, mess management, discipline maintenance, and coordination with college authorities. This position requires strong administrative skills and the ability to handle student-related issues effectively.

Hostal Superintendent Grade II 2024

The Hostal Superintendent Grade II recruitment scheme is designed to select qualified candidates for managing hostel facilities in government educational institutions. The key features of the scheme are:

Key Features

  • Administrative Role: Management of hostel facilities and student welfare
  • Government Job: Permanent position with job security
  • Career Growth: Opportunities for promotion to higher grades
  • Work Environment: Educational institution setting
  • Comprehensive Benefits: Salary, allowances, and retirement benefits

Eligibility Criteria

  • Educational Qualification: Bachelor's degree from a recognized university
  • Age Limit: Generally 18 to 40 years (varies by state and category)
  • Experience: Some positions may require prior experience in hostel management
  • Nationality: Indian citizen
  • Language: Proficiency in local language may be required

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to the most common questions about Hostal Superintendent Grade II (SJED) 2024 recruitment:

What is the eligibility criteria for Hostal Superintendent Grade II (SJED) 2024?

Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university. The age limit is generally 18 to 40 years, with relaxation for reserved categories as per government norms. Some positions may require specific experience in hostel management or educational administration.

What is the selection process for Hostal Superintendent Grade II?

The selection process typically involves a written examination followed by document verification. Some recruitments may include an interview or skill test. The written exam usually consists of multiple-choice questions covering General Knowledge, Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, and English/Hindi language.

What is the salary structure for Hostal Superintendent Grade II?

The salary for Hostal Superintendent Grade II typically falls in Pay Level 6 or 7 as per the 7th Pay Commission, ranging from ‚Çπ35,400 to ‚Çπ1,12,400. The exact salary may vary depending on the state and organization, and includes various allowances like DA, HRA, and medical benefits.

How can I apply for Hostal Superintendent Grade II (SJED) 2024?

Applications are submitted online through the official SJED website. Candidates need to register, fill the application form, upload required documents, and pay the application fee. The exact process will be detailed in the official notification when released.

What is the exam pattern for Hostal Superintendent Grade II?

The exam typically consists of multiple-choice questions divided into sections: General Knowledge, Reasoning Ability, Quantitative Aptitude, English Language, and Hindi Language. The total marks are usually 100-150 with negative marking for wrong answers. The duration is generally 2-3 hours.

Is there any physical requirement for Hostal Superintendent posts?

Generally, there are no specific physical requirements for Hostal Superintendent posts. However, candidates should be medically fit to perform their duties which may involve supervision of hostel facilities, student welfare activities, and administrative tasks.

What are the career growth opportunities for Hostal Superintendent?

Hostal Superintendents can progress to higher grades through departmental promotions. With experience, they can move to positions like Chief Warden, Assistant Director (Hostels), or other administrative roles in the education department. Promotions are typically based on seniority and performance.

Are there any reservations for women candidates?

Yes, there is typically horizontal reservation for women candidates as per government norms. Additionally, some positions may be specifically reserved for female candidates, especially for women's hostels. The exact reservation details are mentioned in the official notification.
