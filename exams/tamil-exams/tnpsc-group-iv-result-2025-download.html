TNPSC Group 4 Result 2025: Complete Analysis

Latest Update: October 22, 2025 | TNPSC Group 4 Result

The Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC) has officially declared the Group 4 Combined Civil Services Examination 2025 results today. Thousands of candidates who appeared for the examination can now check their results on the official website tnpsc.gov.in.

Key Highlights of TNPSC Group 4 Result 2025

  • Result Declaration Date: October 22, 2025
  • Examination Date: July 12, 2025
  • Total Vacancies: 3,935 posts
  • Total Applicants: Over 11.48 lakh candidates
  • Result Status: Written Examination Result
  • Next Stage: Document Verification

How to Check TNPSC Group 4 Result 2025

Follow these simple steps to check your TNPSC Group 4 Result 2025:

  1. Visit the official TNPSC website: tnpsc.gov.in
  2. Click on the "Results" tab in the main navigation
  3. Select "Group 4 Combined Civil Services Examination 2025"
  4. Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth
  5. Click on the "Submit" button
  6. Your result will be displayed on the screen
  7. Download the result PDF for future reference
